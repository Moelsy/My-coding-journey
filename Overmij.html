My daily habits

I firmly believe that habits play a significant role in determining the quality of life. With a desire to maximize my life's potential, I adhere to ten positive habits that promote productivity, health, and happiness.

Choose love: be kind always, because everyone is fighting a battle you don't know about.

Quality time: spend time with your friends and family. I personally enjoy going for long hikes with my family and cooking with my friends.

Exercise: move your body in different ways. And don't forget to stretch.

Eat healthy: drinks water, avoid junk food, eat a lot of fruits and vegetables.

Socialize: meet new people and have fun. I personally love socializing with older people and with people during a surftrip.

Create: I personally love to work on my own coding projects and playing the guitar.

Meditate: Do nothing for 10 minutes. I personally meditate daily and focus on my breath. Not always easy, but it helps to calm my mind.

Learn: I love to learn new things or improve my skills. I try new hobbies, listen to podcast and read books.

Be inspired: re-read quotes and books that inspire you, re-listen podcasts that lift you up. I personally love the podcasts of Lewis Howes from the School of Greatness.

Be present: listen to music, eat slowly, enjoy feeling the sun on your skin or walking in the rain, just observe the world around you.

Rest: Prioritize relaxation and ensure you get sufficient sleep.
